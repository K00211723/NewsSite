Slick design

The following are all examples of design decisions: -What typeface or typeface family to use -What sizes, styles, colors, and weights of those typefaces -How the page or space will lay out (where to place the information) -What shapes to use to enhance that information -What images to use to illustrate that information -What colors to use to reinforce the mood of the message -How big and/or small each element should be -How much clear area (or "white space") to leave around each element
